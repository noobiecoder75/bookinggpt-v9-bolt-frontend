Privacy Policy - BookingGPT

Last updated: January 2025

Important: BookingGPT is committed to protecting your privacy. We only access your Gmail account to send emails on your behalf for travel booking communications.

Information We Collect

BookingGPT accesses your Gmail account only to send emails on your behalf. We collect:

  • Your email address and basic profile information
  • OAuth access tokens to send emails through your Gmail account
  • Email metadata (recipients, subject lines, timestamps) for our records

How We Use Your Information

We use your Gmail access exclusively to:

  • Send travel booking confirmations to your customers
  • Send travel itineraries and updates
  • Send follow-up communications related to travel bookings
  • Send custom emails as directed by you through the BookingGPT interface

What We Don't Do

  • We never read your existing emails
  • We never access your email inbox or folders
  • We never share your email access with third parties
  • We never send emails without your explicit instruction

Data Storage and Security

  • Your Gmail access tokens are stored securely and encrypted in our Supabase database
  • All data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
  • We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data
  • You can revoke access at any time through your Google Account settings

Data Retention

We retain your OAuth tokens and email metadata only as long as necessary to provide the service. You can request deletion of your data at any time.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Revoke Gmail access at any time
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Access information about how your data is used
  • Update your preferences

Limited Use Disclosure

BookingGPT's use of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page.
